A deployed service without observability is hard to trust and harder to support.

## Observability Layers

At minimum, students should know the purpose of:

- logs
- metrics
- traces
- alerts

## Runtime Operations

Operations thinking includes:

- health checks
- readiness
- graceful shutdown
- incident response

## Runbooks

Runbooks capture repeatable operational knowledge such as:

- how to roll back
- how to rotate credentials
- how to handle downstream outage symptoms

## What To Remember

- visibility is part of backend quality
- operations need repeatable procedures
- runbooks reduce chaos during incidents
